About Me

Hello! My name is Drew! I started crocheting in the fall of 2023, and have been addicted ever since. I started by making items of clothing like sweaters, scarves, and headbands, then eventually transitioned into making stuffed animals. I have made dozens of stuffed animals for my friends and family, and am excited to start selling them here in my shop! This site is a place for me to display and sell my crochet creations. I will also start selling crochet patterns on the site sometime soon!

Crochet Essentials

To start crocheting, you need some sort of yarn and a crochet hook. Yarn and crochet hooks have different sizes and textures, best used for specific projects. For my stuffed animals, I use a chunky chenille yarn with a size 8mm crochet hook. This yarn seems to be the softest and coziest for the stuffed animals I want to make. To stuff the animals, I use regular polyester fiber fill that you kind find at any craft store. The eyes and accessories on my animals were all purchased online.
